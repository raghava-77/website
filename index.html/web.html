ABOUT

The Goodwill Student Association Psychotherapy is an
association, formed by Psychology and psychiatry graduate
students, who are in the final years of their study, in Manchester,
UK, to help people with mental issues by counselling and other
support.

Teenage or adolescence issues

Counseling for adolescents and teens, help them deal with their mental issues and improve their functioning at school, home and overall.


Anger Issues

Therapy for anger-related issues - mood swings, violent acts,





Anxiety

Counseling for anxiety disorder and stress - including Generalized Anxiety Disorder, Panic attacks, OCD (Obsessive Compulsive Disorder), PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder) and Social Phobia.

Depression

Therapy for Depression - a condition that results in persistent feelings of despair and sadness, causing loss of interest and interfering with daily functioning.


Marital problems

Counseling for couples or even individuals,seeking solutions or advice for marriage related issues like-intimacy,infidelity,trust issues, boredom,values,beliefs,jeaously,etc.

Suicidal Tendencies

Counseling for people with relentless thoughts of suicide as a result of various life issues.

Our Strategy

We follow certain principles and strategies to connect with and help patients. Our main values are -

Encouragement

We encourage patients to work continuously on their improvement, wihout giving up easily, and push them to keep trying, even if they don’t feel like there is much improvement. Healing and mental health improvement sometimes takes time, and keeping a positive mind through the whole process is one of the main contributing factors to the healing.

Responsibility

We help people understand their own needs better, and work towards their own improvement, and take full responsibility for their successful healing in the end. We only help you see yourself more clearly, so you can decide what you want better, and take responsibility to figure out things fr yourself.

Trust

Building and maintaining trust, a good degree of openness and honesty with our clients is very important to us. We respect all our clients’ privacy equally, and vow to protect it. We consider and value every client’s diverse backgrounds and personalities. We’ll help you build a strong trust with us and yourself, as well as in all your relationships too.
